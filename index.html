JSD Publisher

A private publishing tool for the Jornada Sem Destino motorcycle-travel channel.

JSD Publisher is a private application used by the owner of the Jornada Sem Destino YouTube channel to publish the channel's own original short videos (motovlog and travel footage) to its own TikTok account on a regular schedule.

What it does

  • Takes the channel's finished vertical clips produced by the channel owner.
  • Uploads them to the channel's own TikTok account through TikTok's Content Posting API.
  • Adds the correct caption and hashtags, and posts on a planned cadence.

It is used only by the channel owner, to post the channel's own original content. It does not serve other users and does not collect data from third parties.
